About Sparsh Vaani

A compassionate technology solution built for families.

Sparsh Vaani transforms simple touch gestures into meaningful speech. Designed for children with cerebral palsy, autism, or motor impairments, it provides a simple and intuitive interface to communicate needs, emotions, and thoughts.


It is not just an app — it is a bridge between silence and connection.
